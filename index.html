Welcome

Welcome to Josie and Joe's wedding website. We hope the following information will help you to enjoy our special day.

Directions & Parking

Church Reception

There is car park next to the church. Please be aware that the gates are locked at dusk so we would not advise leaving vehicles there overnight.

There is limited parking at the Italian Villa, when full, cars may be parked on neighbouring roads. Any cars left at the Italian Villa overnight must be moved by 9:30am the following morning.

Hints and Tips

What to Wear

Our winter wedding may be a little chilly so please wrap up warm & bring a brolly or even wellies! Hats and Morning Dress are welcome.

What to Bring

Some dosh for your evening drinks, the bar will accept both cash and cards

Whatever you'll need to keep your baby or toddler happy, there'll be space on the top floor for them to feed, play, and for you to take a break.

When to Eat

If you're invited to the wedding breakfast food will be served at 5pm so be sure to have some lunch before the ceremony.

If you're invited to the evening reception there are some great restaurants and pubs in the local area for some lunch. Close to the church there is the St Peter's Finger, or the Baker's Arms. Nearby the Villa 'Loch Fyne', or try exploring Poole Quay.
